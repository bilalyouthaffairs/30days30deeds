30 Days, 30 Deeds

Ramadan Challenge for Youth & Kids (18 and under)

What is it?

30 Days, 30 Deeds is a Ramadan tracking challenge for children and youth (ages 18 and under). Participants receive a challenge sheet, complete deeds throughout Ramadan, and turn it in for a reward at our Eid events.

How it works

  • Pick up a challenge sheet.
  • Complete deeds throughout Ramadan.
  • Cross out or color in the circle for each completed deed.
  • Turn in your completed sheet at the Eid events.
Three tracks available: Kids Track (Ages 8 and under), Jr. Youth Track (Ages 9-12), and Youth Track (Ages 13-18)
